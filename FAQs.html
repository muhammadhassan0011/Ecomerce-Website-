SHIPPING

HOW MUCH DOES SHIPPING COST?

We offer Free Express Shipping with every order made normally through DHL Express or FedEx.

HOW LONG UNTIL MY ORDER IS SENT OUT?

Our handling time takes 2-4 business days. This means that your order will generally be sent out within this period.

Exceptionally, during Black Friday Sales some orders may take additional processing time due to overwhelming demand.

WHEN WILL I RECEIVE MY ITEMS?

Once we ship your merchandise, our Free Express Shipping method takes around 1-5 business days to deliver worldwide.

DO YOU HAVE ANY SHIPPING INSURANCE?

Yes, Package Protection is automatically added to the cart when you are shopping with us, you can always remove it in case you don't wish to be covered.

This service protects you against stolen, lost or damaged parcels during transit, we higly recommend adding it to your order to avoid any inconviniences.

ORDERS AND PAYMENT

HOW DO I MAKE AN ORDER?

Just click on a product you like and add it to cart. Then follow our easy and secure checkout process.

HOW CAN APPLY MY DISCOUNT COUPON?

You can paste or type your coupon at the checkout page - on the coupon box included in your order summary - the box is below the products you want to buy. After pasting the code click on the "Apply" button and the discount will be deducted from your purchase value.

Please note that you can only use one coupon per order since our discount coupons are not cumulative.

WHAT IS YOUR STORE'S CURRENCY?

All transactions and payments are processed in USD, however you can check the price conversion to your country's currency in the converter on the menu bar.

WHAT PAYMENT METHODS DO YOU ACCEPT?

We currently accept Afterpay, Apple Pay, Mastercard, Visa and American Express.

All transactions are in US Dollars.

All payments made on www.lookslikesummer.com are secure - we use the Secure Sockets Layer protocol (SSL) with an encryption key length of 128-bits (the highest level commercially available) - and we do not store any of your payment information.

TRACKING

HOW DO I TRACK MY ORDER?

You can check the location of your order by entering your tracking number on the TRACK YOUR ORDERpage.

WHEN WILL MY TRACKING NUMBER BECOME ACTIVE?

Usually the tracking numbers will be active within 1-2 business days after the shipping confirmation email is sent. Please note that the tracking activity can suffer delays from customs and shipping processes.

RETURN AND EXCHANGES

WHAT IS YOUR RETURNS POLICY?

We accept returns on Shoes and Handbags within 30 days of the purchase date, or 15 days after the arrival of the merchandise.

We accept returns in the United States, Canada, and the EU - Orders from other countries/zones are considered Final Sale and, thus, are not eligible for the return/exchange process.

Please make sure the merchandise is returned in the exact condition in which it was shipped, new and unused in order to receive a refund. This includes the original packaging and the box it came in.

For more details please visit our Returns & Exchanges page

DO YOU OFFER EXCHANGES?

Yes, we offer free exchanges in the United States, Canada, Pakistan and China.

HOW DO I START A RETURN?

You can start your return process here.

To start the process simply add your order number and email used to make the purchase.

WHEN WILL I RECEIVE MY REFUND?

Once your return is received and inspected, the refund will be processed to the original payment method, during Sales Season please allow up to 10 business days for our team to process your refund once we receive your merchandise.

PRODUCTS

ARE YOUR PRODUCTS MANUFACTURED ETHICALLY?

All our products are made by happy human beings. We are committed to fair employment practices and the upholding of human rights.

WHAT MATERIALS DO YOU USE?

We believe that only through the best raw materials we are able to achieve the most amazingly unique products. For that reason, we maintain a sustainable long-lasting relation with only the best and most reliable suppliers. The material for every model is explicit in the product description.
